Floom is a video conferencing app designed of teachers.

Floom is a video conferencing app with a built-in system for detecting cheating and paying attention. Designed for tests and school presentation, Floom analyzes video feeds to look for behaviors such as switching tabs or windows, and provides a dashboard for teachers to check which students are on task during presentations, and which students are cheating during tests.

Floom was designed by Steven Rachmanchik, Jason Jiang, Gabriel Thompson, and Lucas Lee, for MIT Blueprint 2021.
